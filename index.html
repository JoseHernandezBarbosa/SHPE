What is SHPE?

The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers is the largest Hispanic Engineering and Science organization across the U.S. and Puerto Rico. SHPE-Dartmouth was established in 2018 in order to help develop LatinX students’ professional and academic abilities by offering them academic, networking, advising, and diversity awareness opportunities.

Our Mission

To Create a community that provides support, advice, and guidance through each stage of the Dartmouth career. The future of SHPE is the academic, emotional, professional and social success of marginalized and disadvantaged students


Join Us!

We always welcome new members! Despite our name including “Hispanic Professional Engineers” we accept students of any background and of any STEM major! Please email us for information on how to join.
